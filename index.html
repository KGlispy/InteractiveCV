Values & Community Connection

Exploring my alignment with core organizational values.

My professional journey and personal commitment align deeply with the Olympia Tumwater Foundation's dedication to service, innovation, sustainability, honesty, and recognition.

Service

My approach to **service** involves directly understanding stakeholder needs and proactively addressing them to foster collaborative success. For instance, in a healthcare organization, I introduced DevOps, a methodology aimed at accelerating software delivery. Recognizing team resistance to automation, I built trust by first streamlining their existing workflows, demonstrating value and empowering them with new skills *before* full implementation. This approach is fundamental to successful organizational change and effective stakeholder management.

Innovation

My drive for **innovation** enables the adoption of new capabilities. When faced with introducing a new, highly specialized database design at an insurance company, I developed a unique methodology. This approach strategically leveraged existing team members' deep domain expertise, allowing them to adapt to new technologies through stepped on-ramping and peer programming. This creative solution fostered continuous learning and maximized our collective potential, ensuring internal talent could apply their knowledge effectively.

Sustainability

My commitment extends to **sustainability**, evident in my active participation in environmental preservation efforts that support long-term ecological health. I've volunteered to help eradicate invasive species from Oregon trailheads, understanding this directly contributes to native plant vitality and the lasting well-being of shared natural spaces.

Honesty and Integrity

My commitment to **honesty and integrity** guides critical decisions. In a healthcare organization, during the installation of a new application for oncologists, I made the difficult but necessary choice to roll back a flawed implementation after an intensive weekend effort. Prioritizing long-term quality over immediate costs and convenience, I transparently communicated the rationale, demonstrating that upholding quality standards ensures optimal outcomes and builds trust.

Recognition

I also emphasize **recognition** in leadership. In my roles, I fostered a culture of appreciation by regularly highlighting team and individual achievements. I solicited nominations from peers, ensuring recognition came from those who witnessed daily effort, reinforcing shared value and appreciation within the team.
